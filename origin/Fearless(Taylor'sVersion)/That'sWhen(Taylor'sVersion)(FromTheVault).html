Writer(s): Brett Daniel Warren, Taylor Alison Swift, Brad D. Warren
"That's When" was originally written for Taylor Swift's 2008 album "Fearless" but the song didn't make it to the final tracklist. The demo version of the song, co-written with songwriting duo the Warren Brothers, had only Swift's vocals whilst 2021 version of the song, titled "That's When (Taylor's Version) (From The Vault)", is a duet with Keith Urban. She said on Twitter about working with him, "I'm really honored that Keith Urban is a part of this project, duetting on That's When and singing harmonies on We Were Happy. I was his opening act during the Fearless album era and his music has inspired me endlessly."
