Writer(s): Tommy Lee James, Taylor Alison Swift
"Don't You" was an unreleased track from the times of Taylor Swift's 2008 "Fearless" album. She and Jack Antonoff made a modern version of it for the inclusion to her 2021 "Fearless (Taylor's Version)" album. The song tells a story about running into somebody's former lover and the memories it brings.
