"Bye Bye Baby (Taylor's Version) (From The Vault)" was originally titled "One Thing" when it was still an unreleased track. Taylor Swift changed the lyrics and the title for its official release on her "Fearless (Taylor's Version)" album, a re-recorded version of her 2008 "Fearless" album.
