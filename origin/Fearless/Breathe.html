Writer(s): Colbie Caillat, Taylor Alison Swift
Colbie Caillat is featuring on this track as the co-writer and backing vocals. Taylor Swift co-produced this song with Nathan Chapman.
Taylor Swift said to That's Country, "A song about having to say goodbye to somebody, but it never blames anybody. Sometimes that's the most difficult part. When it's nobody's fault."
Colbie Caillat and Taylor Swift meeting was explained by Swift to That's Country, "I was a huge fan of Colbie's record. When it came out, I fell in love with the way that she makes music. I contacted her management right away and I asked if I could write with her and, sure enough, Colbie had a date coming up in Nashville where she was going to be playing a show and she had the day off."
"Breathe" was nominated for the Grammy Award for Best Pop Collaboration with Vocals, but lost to another collaboration between Colbie Caillat and Jason Mraz, "Lucky".
