Writer(s): Rose Liz, Swift Taylor Alison
The second single from Taylor Swift's second studio album.
The first verse of this song was written solely by Taylor Swift. The rest of the song came out with the help of Liz Rose, co-writer of most track of Swift's debut album.
Taylor Swift described this track by saying, "To me, 'White Horse' is about what, in my opinion, is the most heart-breaking part of a break-up ‚Äì that moment when you realize that all the dreams you had, all those visions you had of being with this person, all that disappears."
"White Horse" debuted on the fifth season premiere of Grey's Anatomy, "Dream a Little Dream of Me", on the 25th of September 2008.
The theme of the music video was explained by Taylor Swift: "This girl falls in love with this guy and he's perfect. He's adorable. He's charming. He's endearing. She falls in love with him. Then, she comes to realize that he's been leading a double life."
"White Horse" won the Grammy Awards for Best Country Song and Best Female Country Vocal Performance at the 2010 Grammy Awards. It was certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America.
