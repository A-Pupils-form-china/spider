"Cardigan" is the lead single from Taylor Swift's eighth studio album "Folklore". The song was premiered on July 24, 2020, accompanied by a music video which was written and directed by Swift herself. Since the filming was done during the COVID-19 pandemic, she commented on Twitter on the 23rd of July 2020, "The entire shoot was overseen by a medical inspector, everyone wore masks, stayed away from each other, and I even did my own hair, makeup, and styling."
Taylor Swift explained the meaning of "Cardigan" by saying, "The song is about a lost romance and why young love is often fixed so permanently within our memories. Why it leaves such an indelible mark."
