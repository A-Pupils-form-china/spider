Writer(s): Dessner Aaron Brooking, Swift Taylor A
"The Last Great American Dynasty" is about the life of Rebekah Harkness, a philanthropist and patron of the arts in the middle of the 20th century. Taylor Swift bought a Rhode Island mansion that had belonged to Harkness previously.
Salvador Dali, mentioned in these lyrics, was a friend of Rebekah Harkness and even made a gold vessel known as "Chalice of Life" at her request. The vessel eventually was used as an urn for Harkness' ashes when she died.
