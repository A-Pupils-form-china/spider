"Only The Young" was released on 01/31/2020, simultaneously with the Netflix documentary "Taylor Swift: Miss Americana". Swift commented on the song, "I don't see it as a single. I just see it as a song that goes with this film. I don't see it as a 'let's go make a music video and try to see what this does at radio' single."
Taylor Swift explained in an interview for Variety that she had written this song after USA 2018 midterm elections. She said in particular, "I saw a lot of young people's hopes dashed. And I found that to be particularly tragic, because young people are the people who feel the worst effects of gun violence, and student loans and trying to figure out how to start their lives..."
