Writer(s): Andrew Lloyd Webber, Taylor Alison Swift
Taylor Swift, who co-wrote this song with Andrew Lloyd Webber specifically for the film adaptation of the musical "Cats", recorded the radio version of "Beautiful Ghosts".
In the film, this song is performed by Victoria, the kitten portrayed by Francesca Hayward. Victoria responds with it to "Memory" sung by Grizabella right before "Beautiful Ghosts" in the film.
Director Tom Hooper told about "Beautiful Ghosts", "What I couldn't believe was the extraordinary beauty of these lyrics that Taylor had written. And it was as if someone would come into the film I was in the middle of making and—you know—read the script and met the actors, and seen the world, and reflected back to me a—and—a profound understanding of what we were trying to do with the movie, so... it was utterly thrilling."
