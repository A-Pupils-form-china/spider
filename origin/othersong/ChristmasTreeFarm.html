Writer(s): Irving Berlin
Taylor Swift revealed in a video on Twitter on the 5th of December 2019, that she had written a new song which would later become known as "Christmas Tree Farm". In order to decide if she should put it out she asked three of her cats.
This song was announced on "Good Morning America" on 12/05/2019. The music video, made of home videos, was published on the same day.
This song was written just four days before its release as a stand-alone single on the 6th of December 2019.
