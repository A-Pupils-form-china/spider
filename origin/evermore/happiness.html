Writer(s): Dessner Aaron Brooking, Swift Taylor A
Taylor Swift wrote about "Happiness", "The realization that maybe the only path to healing is to wish happiness on the one who took it away from you."
This song was the last one written for the album, finished just a week before its release.
The line "I hope she'll be a beautiful fool" refers to the novel "The Great Gatsby" (1925), where the term "beautiful fool" was used.
