Writer(s): Taylor Alison Swift, Jack Antonoff
'Soon You'll Get Better' is dedicated to Taylor Swift's mother. The singer explained to Elle Magazine, "My mom's cancer has taught me that there are real problems and then there's everything else. My mom's cancer is a real problem. I used to be so anxious about daily ups and downs. I give all of my worry, stress, and prayers to real problems now."
A picture of Dixie Chicks, which are featured as background vocals on this song, can be seen on a wall in the music video for 'Me!'.
