Writer(s): Taylor Alison Swift, Jack Antonoff
'The Archer' is the first promotional single for her album 'Lover' (2019). The song was released on the 23rd of July 2019.
There is no music video for this song because Taylor Swift didn't plan it to be a single. She said the song was meant to show another side of the album, unlike the first two singles having been released earlier: 'Me!' and 'You Need to Calm Down'.
'The Archer' is the fifth track off of the album. Taylor Swift explained during a live stream, "Instinctively I was putting a very vulnerable, personal, honest, emotional song as track five." She also added, "This is a glimpse into another side of the album I wanted to show you."
This track samples the nursery rhyme 'Humpty Dumpty' with the lines "All the king's horses, all the king's men".
