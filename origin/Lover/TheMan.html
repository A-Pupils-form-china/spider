Writer(s): Taylor Alison Swift, Jack Antonoff
"The Man" imagines its protagonist's life as though she were male and how the public would perceive her differently.
The song references Leonardo DiCaprio and St. Tropez, where he hosts yearly galas and is often seen surrounded by young and beautiful models. The lyric implies he's never called out for this, while Swift has been scrutinised in the past for the number of men she's been in relationships with.
