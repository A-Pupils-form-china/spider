Writer(s): Taylor Alison Swift
Taylor Swift explained this song by writing on Spotify Enhanced, "I chose 'Daylight' as the last song on the album because it recognises past damage and pain, but shows that it doesn't have to define you. For me, the 'reputation' album seemed like nighttime. The 'Lover' album feels completely sunlit. I wrote this one alone."
The beginning of the second verse was revealed during the Spotify Love Letters promotional campaign for the album.
