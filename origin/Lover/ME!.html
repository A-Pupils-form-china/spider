Writer(s): Joel Little, Brendon Urie, Taylor Alison Swift
"ME!" was premiered on April 26, 2019, accompanied by music video.
"This is a song about embracing your individuality and really celebrating it, and owning it," said Taylor Swift announcing the single.
She adds about the collaboration with Panic! At The Disco frontman: "Brendon has always been one of my favorite performers and I've always had it in the back of my mind that it would be insane to find the right thing to collab on and when I wrote this chorus I knew."
The video broke YouTube record for most views in the first 24 hours for a lead female video, earning 65.2 million views.
