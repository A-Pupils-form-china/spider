Writer(s): Taylor Alison Swift
Cornelia Street is a street in Manhattan in New York City. She told Elvis Duran during his morning show about the song, "It's about the things that took place, the memories that were made on that street. I rented an apartment there, and just wanted to write a song about all the nostalgia of, you know, sometimes in our lives we assign, you know, we kind of bond our memories to those places where those memories happened."
