Writer(s): Taylor Alison Swift, Jack Antonoff
Taylor Swift was inspired to write "Death By A Thousand Cuts" after watching a Netflix movie "Someone Great". She told during Elvis Duran's radio show on the 23rd of August 2019, "I think the movie is going to be like, 'Get over him girl! He's a jerk! Let's take shots.' I was like, 'I'm down. Let's watch this movie.'" Jenn Kaytin Robinson, who directed the film, drew inspiration for it while listening to Swift's song 'Clean' from the album '1989'.
