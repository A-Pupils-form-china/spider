Writer(s): Swift Taylor
The lead single from her third studio album.
In an interview with Rolling Stone, Taylor Swift revealed the source of inspiration for this song by saying, "I was reflecting back on a boy I liked at a certain time."
During a live Ustream chat with fans on July 20, 2010, Taylor Swift explained, "I think I've developed this pattern of sort of running away when it comes time to fall in love and to stay in a relationship. The song is sort of about finding the exception to that and finding someone who would make you believe in love and realize that it could work out."
The song was sent to country radio and iTunes twelve days earlier than planned, on the 4th of August 2010, because the song leaked in an MP3-file before the official release date.
This track entered the US Billboard Hot 100 at number three. On August 21, 2014, the song was certified triple platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America. It was also a top-ten hit in Australia, Canada and Japan. The Australian Recording Industry Association certified it Gold.
British actor Toby Hemingway is featured in the music video for "Mine". He was chosen by Taylor Swift after watching "Feast of Love" (2007) film, where he played a role. The video received the Video of the Year Award at the CMT Music Awards in 2011.
The music video was shot in Kennebunkport, Maine, and Taylor Swift premiered it there to an audience of several hundred local people. Former US President George H. W. Bush and his grandchildren attended the event.
Maroon 5 made a cover of this song.
