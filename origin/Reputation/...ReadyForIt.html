Writer(s): Taylor Swift
The album's second official single.
"Burton to this Taylor" of these lyrics is a reference to Elizabeth Taylor and Richard Burton.
Critic Richard He wrote for Billboard that "Swift has never sung more expressively, nor sounded more in tune with the way modern pop production uses the voice as an instrument".
The music video shows Taylor Swift playing two roles: a prisoner in a sci-fi world and a girl who watches a prisoner.
American musician BloodPop was featured on the remix of this song. A video for the remix was uploaded to Taylor Swift's Vevo channel on YouTube. It includes clips from the original music video.
"...Ready For It?" was the first song performed every night at Taylor Swift's Reputation Stadium Tour.
