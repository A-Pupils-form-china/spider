Taylor Swift's "…Ready For It?" music video shows a wall in the background with a red graffiti crowned heart which is, apparently, a hint at this song's title. The same video also had a writing on the wall "this is enough" which is a part of these lyrics.
The words "Motown beat" is a reference a style of pop-influenced soul music called "Motown Sound".
