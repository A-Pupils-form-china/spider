Taylor Swift explained this song to iHeart Radio, "It's about when people take nice things for granted. Like friendship, or trusting people, or being open or whatever. Letting people in on your life, trusting people, respect – those are all really nice things."
The first verse of this track references Taylor Swift parties she was throwing up until 2017 with many of famous people invited, including Ed Sheeran, Beyoncé, Justin Timberlake, and Lorde.
