Writer(s): Taylor Swift
Taylor Swift co-produced this song with Nathan Chapman and Dann Huff.
The second official single from Red released on the 1st of October 2012. It was initially released as a promotional single on the 25th of September 2012.
The song was previewed on Good Morning America on the 24th of September 2012.
Taylor Swift described, "It's actually a song about when you've gotten through a really bad relationship and you finally dust yourself off and go on that first date after a horrible break-up."
The Boot website described this track writing, "It's a beautiful ballad that tells the story of a girl who is pleasantly surprised by a new guy who does everything right that the last guy did wrong."
These lyrics mention James Taylor, a country singer who once joined Taylor Swift on stage during her 2011 Speak Now World Tour to perform his song "Fire and Rain".
The song's music video was filmed in Paris in September 2012. The male model who plays Taylor Swift's new love interest in the video was chosen by her because he had "good acting instincts" and "a really sweet and endearing charm to him", as she told MTV UK News.
The single has been certified Platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America.
