Writer(s): Taylor Swift
"I Almost Do" is about missing a former lover and reminiscing about a past relationship. Taylor Swift said about it in her Paint the World Red website, "The song 'I Almost Do' is a song I wrote about the conflict that you feel when you want to take someone back and you want to give it another try, but you know you can't. You can't because it's hurt you so deeply that you know that you couldn't bear to go through that again."
This song peaked at number 65 on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart. On the US Country Songs chart, it peaked at number 13.
